Bob Creek Cabin

The Bob Creek Cabin is a surprisingly modern cabin, just across the road from the crashing waves of Bob Creek Beach, a beach famous for its world class agate hunting, tide pools, secret caves, and spectacular sunsets.

This architect-designed cabin is nestled beside Bob Creek, in a forest of spruce, fir and shore pines, providing serenity and a delightful blend of comfort and aesthetic detail. Enjoy cooking in the cabin’s full kitchen and dine al fresco on the outdoor deck, or cozy up to the Morso wood burning stove. This stylish cabin has all the comforts of home with WIFI, smart TVs, board games, a shuffleboard table, and a hot tub.

The Bob Creek Cabin is located 5 miles south of downtown Yachats, on the Siuslaw National Forest, with countless outdoor activity choices. On the property, explore Bob Creek and possibly spot its elusive river otter family, or stroll across the street to the beach to explore tide pools and catch the sunset. The surrounding area is abundant in scenic hiking trails and must-see spots such as Thor’s Well (2.6 mi), Cape Perpetua (2.9 mi), Devil's Churn (3.4 mi) and the iconic Heceta Head Lighthouse (8.5 mi). At the Bob Creek Cabin, you’ll discover why visitors and locals alike find Yachats to be a magical place.
